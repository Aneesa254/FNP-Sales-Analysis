# 📊 Ferns and Petals (FNP) Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📝 Project Description
This project presents a comprehensive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel using data from *Ferns and Petals (FNP)*, a company specializing in gifting products for various occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine’s Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into **meaningful insights** that help in understanding sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends, ultimately supporting better business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales performance  
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Examine delivery efficiency  
- Compare sales across occasions and time periods  

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview
The dataset includes:
- Order details  
- Product information  
- Customer data  
- Order and delivery dates  
- Occasion categories  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation & Cleaning
- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing and inconsistent values  
- Standardized date formats and categorical fields  
- Created calculated columns:
  - **Revenue = Price × Quantity**
  - **Delivery Time = Delivery Date – Order Date**  

- Applied Excel functions:
  - `XLOOKUP`  
  - `IF`  
  - `TEXT`  
  - Date functions  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Development
An interactive dashboard was built using:

- **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts**
- **Slicers** for dynamic filtering:
  - Order Date  
  - Delivery Date  
  - Occasion  

### 🔑 KPI Metrics
- Total Orders  
- Total Revenue  
- Average Customer Spend  
- Average Delivery Time  

---

## 📈 Business Analysis & Insights

### 1. Total Revenue
- Calculated total revenue generated from all orders  

### 2. Average Order & Delivery Time
- Evaluated operational efficiency in order fulfillment  

### 3. Monthly Sales Performance (2023)
- Identified peak and low-performing months  

### 4. Top Products by Revenue
- Highlighted products contributing the most to revenue  

### 5. Customer Spending Analysis
- Determined average customer spend per order  

### 6. Sales Performance of Top 5 Products
- Compared contribution of best-selling products  

### 7. Top 10 Cities by Orders
- Identified regions with highest demand  

### 8. Order Quantity vs Delivery Time
- Analyzed relationship between order volume and delivery delays  

### 9. Revenue by Occasion
- Compared performance across different occasions  

### 10. Product Popularity by Occasion
- Identified which products perform best for specific events  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Sales peak during **Anniversary** and **Raksha Bandhan**  
- A few products contribute significantly to total revenue  
- Evening hours show higher customer activity  
- Certain categories outperform others consistently  
- Delivery time increases slightly with higher order volumes
